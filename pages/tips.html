Några förslag på hur man kan förbättre tillgängligheten

Minimum Text Size

WCAG 2.0 suggests using text no smaller than 16pt.

Alt Tags + Captions

Media should have descriptive text for screen readers.

Element Contrast

Ensure the colors and font weights and sizes have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1.

Alternatives to Complex Actions

Provide a method for skipping over long lists of links or other lengthy content.

Focus States

Provide focus states for all interactive elements and text on the page for users relying on keyboard navigation.

More White Space

Utilize empty space on the page to break up sections and focus in on content. Too much content can overstimulate and confuse users.
